Interview: Albert Dupontel

Dejan Ćirić     26/8/2018

Albert Dupontel, director of "See you up there" said that Belgrade Film Fest is one of the best that he saw, and that Serbia is country that he will gladly return to, primarily because of people he met.
"Beeing in Serbia is something that I will remember till the end of my life, I'm grateful to crew of BFF for inviting me and I*m glad that my movie will be shown for Serbian audience" he said.
