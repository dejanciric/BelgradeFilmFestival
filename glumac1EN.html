Name: Antonio Banderas
Date of birth: 10.8.1960.
Country of birth: Spain
Movie: Black Butterfly

Biography

Bandera was born on 10 August 1960, in the Andalusian city of Málaga, to José Domínguez Prieto, a police officer in the Civil Guard, and Ana Bandera Gallego, a school teacher. As a child, he wanted to become a professional soccer player until a broken foot sidelined his dreams at the age of fourteen. His work in the theater and his performances on the streets, eventually landed him a spot with the Spanish National Theatre. He began his acting career with a series of films by director Pedro Almodóvar and key part was in Tie Me Up! Tie Me Down (šp. Átame!), in 1990 with Victoria Abril. After that he went to USA and stared in movies including Mambo Kings, Philadelphia,Interview with the Vampire, Desperado, Assassins ect.
