Biography

Nahuel Pérez Biscayart was born on March 6, 1986 in Buenos Aires, Federal District, Argentina. Herew up in Buenos Aires's neighborhood Parque Chas. He studied acting with Nora Moseinco. He is an actor and director, known for Glue (2006), BPM (Beats Per Minute) (2017) and All Yours (2014).
