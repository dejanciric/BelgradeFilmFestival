Buč Kesidi were the last preformers on 9. BFF

Young bend from Pančevo had the honor to play their music on closing night of last years Belgrade Film Fest. In Serbian capital Belgrade, in front of cuople thousend people, they played songs from their album "Posesivno-ospulsivni hospul". Hits like "Jaa (imam plaan)", "Draga" i "Kafe aparat" were echoing in Dom omladine in Belgrade in celebration of ending festival. "We are glad that we had the honor to be the last one to play at BFF, atmosphere was magnificant" - said frontman Luka Racić.

Last film night with Serbian movie "Panama"

Last day of Belgrade Film Fest is not the day for competiton movies but for domestic movies with big potential. Honor to be the last movie to br played on 9. BFF was given to movie "Panama" directed by Pavle Vučković, which got great reviews. With young actors Jovan Došlo, Miloš Pjevač and Jovana Stojiljković as lead roles this movie has blown away domestic audience on its premiere three days ago, and is set to do the same on closing day od ninth BFF.

Award winners declared

As the competition part of BFF is done, the last day is saved for declaring winners. Jury which consist of Simona Nobile, Jelena Angelovski, Vladan Petković and Marko Grba has watched all the movies and made their decision which actors and directors will get awarded. Rewards are, as always, split in two categories, "Lighthouse" for feature-length movies (only for best movie) and "Early works" for short movies in which there are more categories.



Award Lighthouse:

Best movie: Popstar (Aleksa Brkić)

Award Early works:

Best movie: The room (Petar Đekanovć)
Best director: Zoran Zarubica (7 days in hell)
Best screenplaz: Vanja Cvejić (The turnament)
Best actress: Bojana Stanić (New girl)
Best actor: Miloš Matijašević (Neighbours)
